How It Works

Three steps to your next foursome

Scramble makes it easy to find golfers who match your game, schedule, and vibe.

1

Build Your Profile

Tell us how you play. Your pace, style, preferred tees, betting habits, availability, and what you're looking for in a golf partner.

2

Get Matched

Each week, Scramble builds your lineup of compatible golfers nearby. Browse profiles, check match scores, and see what you have in common.

3

Hit the Links

Message your matches, form a foursome, book a tee time, and get out there. It's that simple.
